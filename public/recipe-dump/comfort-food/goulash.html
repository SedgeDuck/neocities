Goulash

Description

Family comfort food recipe.

Ingredients

  • Finished Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1-2 bell peppers, chopped (optional)
  • 1 lb. box rotini, macaroni, or other short pasta
  • 8 oz. cheddar, shredded

Method

  1. Optional: add chopped peppers to sauce during meat browning stage.
  2. Add water to make sauce soupy and bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles and bring back to a boil.
  4. Reduce to low heat, cover, and cook 15-20 minutes.
  5. Stir in cheese to taste and serve.
