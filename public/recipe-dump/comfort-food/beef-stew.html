Beef Stew

Description

Family comfort food recipe.

Ingredients

  • Beef roast, chopped OR stew meat
  • Flour
  • Seasoning salt and pepper
  • Oil
  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Other stew veg (if on-hand)
  • Chicken or beef broth

Method

  1. Season flour with salt and pepper and coat meat.
  2. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Brown sides of meat chunks, then transfer to slow cooker.
  3. Add vegetables and broth, and cook for 6-8 hours.
