Description

This one is a weird one. Super cheap, super easy, and slightly less bad than it sounds. Desperation food.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 3 cans tomato soup
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder
  • Salt and minced onions to taste

Method

  1. Brown and drain ground beef.
  2. Mix in soup and seasonings.
  3. Cook noodles separately (or water down sauce and cook in same pot).
