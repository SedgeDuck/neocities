Spaghetti Sauce

Description

Family comfort food recipe.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup veg oil
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup popcorn kernels

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large nonstick pot.
  2. Cover and shake on burner over medium-high heat until popping slows down/stops.
  3. Immediately remove from heat and pour away from body into a large bowl.
