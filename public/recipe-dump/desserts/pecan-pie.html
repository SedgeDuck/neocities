Goulash

Description

pie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs, room temp
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup pecans, chopped

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In a large bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Stir in melted butter.
  3. Add brown sugar, white sugar, and flour; mix well.
  4. Add milk, vanilla, and nuts.
  5. Pour into an unbaked 9" pie shell. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce to 300° for 50-55 minutes, or until center is set.
