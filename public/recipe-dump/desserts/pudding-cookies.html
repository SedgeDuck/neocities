Pudding Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Even chewier than the regular ones!!!

Ingredients

  • 4½ cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cup butter, softened
  • 1½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 pkg instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 4 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 cup chopped walnuts

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Sift together flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars.
  4. Beat in pudding mix, eggs, and vanilla.
  5. Slowly add flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased sheet pan.
  7. Bake 10-12 minutes until edges are golden brown.
  8. Cookies will set when cooled.
