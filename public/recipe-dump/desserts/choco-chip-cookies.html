Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chewy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 cup flour
  • 2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Mix in baking soda and salt. Slowly mix in flour.
  5. Add chocolate chips and nuts. Drop dough balls ~½ inch apart on ungreased pan.
  6. Bake ~10 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  7. Cookies will set once cooled.
