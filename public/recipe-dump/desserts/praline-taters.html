Praline Sweet Potatoes

Description

Marshmallow sweet potato pie is gross. These are much better.

Ingredients

  • 4 cup sweet potatoes, boiled and mashed (approx. 6-8 potatoes)
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 2 Tbsp vanilla
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¼ lb butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 ¼ cup pecans, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Butter a 9x13" pan.
  2. Combine sweet potatoes, white sugar, vanilla, eggs, and cream. Blend well and spread evenly in dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine butter, brown sugar, flour, and pecans. Cut together until crumbly and sprinkle over sweet potato mixture.
  4. Bake uncovered 35-60 minutes until topping is crisp on the edges and firm in the middle.
